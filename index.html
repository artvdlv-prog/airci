Airchitect - лучший способ управления строительством инфраструктуры, от закупок до управления проектами.

AirControl

Comprehensive project oversight through AI-powered auditing, real-time IoT sensor integration, drone surveillance, and predictive risk analysis. Monitor construction progress, identify potential issues before they become critical, and maintain complete visibility across all project phases with automated reporting and alerts.

AirMarket

Intelligent marketplace integration that automatically searches for relevant tenders, matches optimal equipment and suppliers based on project requirements, and generates professional applications with AI assistance. Streamlines procurement processes and ensures competitive bidding with automated proposal creation and vendor evaluation.

AirLegal

Advanced legal AI assistant that reviews contracts for risks and compliance issues, automatically generates claims and legal documents, monitors regulatory changes, and ensures all project activities meet legal requirements. Reduces legal costs and minimizes compliance risks through proactive monitoring and automated documentation.

AirFlow

Workforce optimization through intelligent HR management, automated RACI matrix generation, and real-time process bottleneck identification. Optimizes team allocation, identifies workflow inefficiencies, and provides actionable insights for improving project delivery timelines and resource utilization.

AirCodex

Intelligent knowledge base with advanced search capabilities across all construction codes, standards, and regulations including SNiP, GOST, and international building codes. Provides instant access to relevant regulations, compliance requirements, and technical specifications with contextual recommendations for your specific projects.

AirAcademy

Immersive learning platform combining VR training experiences with comprehensive online education covering BIM technologies, legal compliance, safety protocols, and technical skills. Provides personalized learning paths, certification tracking, and hands-on simulation training for construction professionals at all levels.

AirSupport

24/7 technical support and consultation services with expert construction professionals. Provides immediate assistance for project challenges, technical questions, and platform optimization through dedicated support channels and real-time collaboration tools.

AirChi

Central AI orchestrator powered by advanced Natural Language Processing that seamlessly integrates all platform modules through conversational interfaces. Acts as your intelligent project companion, understanding natural language queries, coordinating cross-module workflows, and providing unified insights across all construction operations through a single, intuitive user experience.
